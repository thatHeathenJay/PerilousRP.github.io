General Roleplay Rules

Perilous Roleplay Official Handbook

Roleplay thrives on structure, respect, and a shared commitment to immersion. These rules are comprehensive, addressing behavior, engagement, and immersion to create a fair and enjoyable community environment. Violations of the rules outlined in this handbook can result in Verbal Warnings (VW) and/or Warning Points (WP). Accumulating WP may lead to temporary or permanent bans, as detailed in the Warning Points System section. Specific WP ranges for violations are listed at the end of each section's introduction.

Section 0: Common Sense

The foundation of a thriving roleplay community is mutual respect, integrity, and adherence to both the spirit and the letter of the rules. While this handbook outlines comprehensive guidelines, no rule system can account for every possible scenario. Players are expected to exercise common sense and good judgment in all situations. Exploiting rule gaps or engaging in behavior that feels inherently wrong undermines the community and will not be tolerated.

If a player asks themselves, "Is this a rule?" or "Will this get me in trouble?" they are encouraged to seek clarification from Staff or err on the side of caution. Violations of this principle, including exploiting loopholes or engaging in questionable conduct, may result in Warning Points (WP), Verbal Warnings (VW), or other administrative actions, even if the behavior is not explicitly outlined in this handbook. Administrative punishments are determined by Staff at their discretion, based on the severity and necessity of the situation.

The WP system tracks violations to ensure consistent enforcement of rules. WP accumulate over time and may result in temporary or permanent bans. However, players can reduce their WP by demonstrating improved behavior. For every month a player remains free of administrative action, 10 WP will be deducted from their total.

0.1 Common Sense Guidelines

  • If an action feels unrealistic, unfair, or immersion-breaking, assume it is against the rules unless clarified otherwise.
  • Do not exploit rule gaps or technicalities to justify behavior that would otherwise be inappropriate.
  • When in doubt, consult with Staff through appropriate OOC channels to ensure compliance with community standards.

0.2 Admin Discretion

  • Staff reserve the right to issue WP, VW, or other disciplinary actions for conduct that violates the spirit of the rules or disrupts the community, regardless of whether the specific behavior is explicitly stated in this handbook.
  • Admin discretion will be exercised thoughtfully, taking into account context, intent, and impact on the community.

0.3 Player Accountability

  • Players are responsible for understanding and adhering to the rules, as well as asking for clarification when needed.
  • Ignorance of the rules or failure to exercise common sense will not be considered a valid defense.

About the San Andreas State Police

The San Andreas State Police (SASP) is a premier law enforcement agency dedicated to maintaining public safety and upholding the law across the state of San Andreas. Established to serve and protect the diverse communities within the state, the SASP is committed to professionalism, integrity, and excellence in all its operations.

With a team of highly trained officers, the SASP provides a wide range of services, including traffic enforcement, criminal investigations, emergency response, and community outreach. Our mission is to ensure that every resident and visitor of San Andreas feels secure and confident in the safety of their environment.

We believe in fostering strong partnerships with local communities, other law enforcement agencies, and public service organizations to enhance the quality of life for all. Through rigorous training, advanced technology, and a commitment to justice, the San Andreas State Police strive to be a model of effective and ethical law enforcement.

About the San Andreas Fire and Rescue

San Andreas Fire and Rescue (SAFR) is a dedicated emergency services organization serving the state of San Andreas. SAFR is committed to protecting life, property, and the environment through comprehensive fire suppression, rescue operations, and emergency medical services. With a team of highly trained firefighters, paramedics, and support personnel, SAFR responds to a wide range of emergencies, including structural fires, vehicle accidents, medical emergencies, hazardous material incidents, and natural disasters.

Operating out of multiple fire stations strategically located throughout San Andreas, SAFR is equipped with state-of-the-art fire engines, ambulances, rescue vehicles, and specialized equipment to handle any situation. SAFR members undergo rigorous training to ensure they are prepared for all scenarios, from firefighting and technical rescues to advanced life support and disaster response.

SAFR is committed to community safety, continuously engaging in public education, fire prevention initiatives, and community outreach programs to reduce the risk of emergencies and enhance public awareness. Through collaboration with other emergency services and agencies, SAFR strives to maintain the highest standards of service, professionalism, and excellence in protecting the residents and visitors of San Andreas.
